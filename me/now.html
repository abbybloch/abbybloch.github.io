What I'm Working on Now:

Since spring semester started, I've been working on my creativity and how I can improve how I work. Because I am going into a creative field, I want to be able to create more consistantly instead of in spurratic bursts like I do now. Other than just all around mental health work, I've been working with "The Artists Way" by Julia Cameron.

Following the book you go week by week and you complete:

  • Morning pages (writing 3 pages every morning about anything)
  • Writing/Reading from the book
  • Artist Dates (once a week you go out alone and do something for your inner artist)
It's interesting to see how working through the writing prompts and reading, is helping in my work in the audio field as a creative.
