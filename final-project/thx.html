This project was completed as an exercise to sound design a clip and do soem effects editing. The sound was stripped from the original clip and replaced with new sounds. The sounds were created using everyday objects, found on Soundly and freesounds.org, and provided by my mentor. Logic soundscape sounds were alto used and edited to create some of the design. The helicopter was created using a free demo of Krotos Igniter.
